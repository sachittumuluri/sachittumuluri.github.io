#about

I build software where signals meet decisions — machine-learning systems, quantitative research tooling, and side projects with far too much personality.

Right now that means Vega Labs, where I'm building AI agents for sales pipelines that keep the judgment in the model and the rules in code. Before that: quant research and backtesting infrastructure with MAIF, and applying topological data analysis to ECG signals to detect ventricular tachycardia.

I like systems that are verifiable, tools that feel like instruments, and READMEs that don't lie.
